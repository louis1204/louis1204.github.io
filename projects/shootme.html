About

This app helps guide people to take a picture exactly how you wanted it.

You first take a shot of a scenery. Then you give the phone to someone as you go stand in the scenery. Arrows guide the person to tilt the phone until the orientation is the same your shoot. The user can also super impose the previous shot over the current camera view to match up the scenery. When all is right the app will do a countdown or the user can click a button to take the picture.
